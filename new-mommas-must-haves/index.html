baby • Jul 28, 2020

* La Vie: Lactation Massager Roller; Stimulate Milk Flow

I would rate this product a 10 out of 10! It works great, makes massaging my breasts before pumping or breastfeeding so much easier.

* La Vie: Warming Lactation Massage Pads; Improve Milk Flow & Save Time, Heat & Vibration.

I would rate this product a definite 10 out of 10! I instantly start to lactate the moment I turn them on prior to starting my breast pump. I also greatly enjoy the warming feature, it helps sooth my sore breasts and nipples especially after my son cluster feeds. The vibration setting is also a plus because it really gets that milk flowing.

* La Vie: Pump Strap Hands Free

I would rate this product a 10 out of 10! Being able to multitask while pumping and or nursing is the best. I have tried several " nursing bras" and I have come to find that this one is the only one that really works. I don't have to worry about holding the bottles in position while I'm pumping because this bra compresses for me. It is also quite comfortable being that it doubles as a halter and strapless and velcro's in the back.
