Ingredients

  • 2 cups of old-fashioned oats
  • 1/2 cup of flaxseed meal
  • 3 tablespoons Brewer's yeast
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

Mix all ingredients except the chocolate chips together using a electric hand mixer until well combined. (insert pic)

Stir in the chocolate chips. (insert pic)

Roll mixture into 2-tablespoon sized balls and place on parchment-lined baking sheet. (insert pic)

Place in Refrigerator for 30 minutes or eat up right away. Once the bites have set, place in an air-tight container and store in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks. (insert pic)
