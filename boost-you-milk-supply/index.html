Boosting your Booby Juice Supply!

Samantha Zygowicz

28 Jul 2020 • 1 min read

Being a first time mom is hard enough, but not having control over the amount of milk you produce can be stressful. These are a few products I have tried and have come to find have been a big help in boosting my Booby Juice supply.

Body Armor

10 out of 10 for a yummy way to boost your Booby Juice Supply! Highly recommend getting the 28 oz bottles for only a $1.00 at Festival. I go through these so fast. They have a variety of flavors, Peach Mango is my personal fav!

They can be found on Amazon, at your local Pick N Save, Piggly Wiggly, Festival.

Pink Stork Lactation Tea

This product works great and for that I would rate it a 9 out of 10. If the taste was better I would say a 10 out of 10. I think it needs to be drunk before every pumping to really see good results. I have come to find that if I don't have it for every pump my milk supply isn't as good. You can drink this hot or cold, I highly recommend adding a slice or 2 of lemon. It only comes in 1 flavor Vanilla Chai, the taste is somewhat overpowering and has a strong black licorice undertone. Adding lemon really tones done the overpowering flavors.

This product was purchased on Amazon.

UpSpring Milk Flow Berry Flavored Breastfeeding Supplement Drink

I would rate this drink an 8 out of 10. The taste is not the greatest like my Body Armor but I love that I can add it to shakes and it comes in simple to go packs. This comes in a variety of flavors and can go in smoothies and water.

This product was purchased on Amazon.
